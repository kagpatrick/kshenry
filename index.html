Welcome

Public health researcher & epidemiologist

Dr. Henry Sohre Kitiabi is an epidemiologist and biostatistician focused on infectious disease surveillance, HIV prevention (PrEP), tuberculosis control, and data-driven policy. He combines rigorous quantitative methods with global health field experience to inform evidence-based interventions.
